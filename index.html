00.ABOUT ME 

My name is Alex Vetrick and I am a graphic designer, augmented and virtual reality content creator and developer. I use new and traditional mediums to create interesting and interactive art. My past job experiences with NASA and Oculus have added valuable UX/UI skills to my process. I strive to push the limits of technology into my 3D modeling and packaging design. I love to expand on what is considered conventional design with AR/VR tools. I bring an extra dimension to design no matter what the job.

We offer a curated suite of design services specifically tailored for the small business owner. From web design to visual identity, we make it simple for you to establish a robust online presence without the premium price tag.
